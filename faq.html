Useful Information

What if my wristband is lost or does not work ?
Please reach us at 1900 63 69 20 or the receptionist for support in this case.

All The New Gym centers open 24/24?
The majority of our centers open 24/7. Visit our website for details.

In case I've left a personal items in the gym?

Please leave a notification message in our Contact Us tab, declaring details of the item and where it may have been left in the gym. We will always try to return any lost properties to the owner, however, we do not take responsibility for any items carried inside the property. Such items will be kept until claimed within one week. After one week, the items will either be donated to charity or destroyed.

Can I leave my belongings in the locker overnight?
Members are asked to only use their lockers for the duration of training session. Lockers will be emptied at night. All member's items will be moved in to lost properties.
